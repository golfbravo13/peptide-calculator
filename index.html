📖 How to use this calculator

① Dose — This is the amount of peptide you want per injection. Check your protocol for the recommended dose (e.g. 250 mcg = 0.25 mg).

② Strength — This is the total amount of peptide in your vial, printed on the label (e.g. 5 mg, 10 mg).

③ BAC Water — This is how much Bacteriostatic Water (BAC water) or sterile water you will inject into the vial to dissolve the peptide powder.

Result — The calculator tells you how many units to draw on a standard U-100 insulin syringe to get your desired dose.
