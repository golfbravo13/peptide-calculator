📖 How to use this calculator

① Dose — The amount of peptide you want per injection.

② Strength — The total amount of peptide in your vial, printed on the label.

③ BAC Water — How much Bacteriostatic Water you will inject into the vial to dissolve the peptide powder.

Result — The calculator tells you how many units to draw on a standard U-100 insulin syringe.
